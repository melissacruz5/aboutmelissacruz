Get to Know Me

I love to travel

I’ve always loved traveling, especially with my family. We’ve explored many parts of Brazil—like Belém, Salvador, Fortaleza, and São Paulo—and every trip has helped us grow closer while discovering the beauty and diversity of our own country. We’ve also been lucky to visit places like Italy, Spain, Portugal, and the United States. My favorite trip was to Italy—I fell in love with the culture, the architecture, the food, and the breathtaking views. Experiencing it all with my family made it even more special. Traveling isn’t just something we do for fun—it’s how we create memories, learn, and grow together.

I love to listen to music

Music has always been a big part of my life. I grew up listening to 70s and 80s pop and rock, especially Brazilian rock from the 80s. My dad passed down his favorite songs to me, and some of my best memories are dancing around the house with him and my sister. My music taste is basically a love letter to those moments. My favorite band is Legião Urbana, and my all-time favorite song is “Giz.” You can watch a performance of it below!
